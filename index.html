Privacy Policy

ScanHa Mobile Application

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ScanHa collects, uses, stores, protects, and shares information when you download, access, or use the ScanHa mobile application.

By using ScanHa, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please stop using the application.

2. About ScanHa

ScanHa is a food and product guidance application that helps users check product information by scanning barcodes, viewing available product data, and accessing allergy or dietary-related guidance.

ScanHa is an informational tool only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Users should always check the original product packaging and consult a qualified healthcare professional when needed.

3. Information We May Collect

Depending on how you use the application, ScanHa may collect or process the following types of information:

  • Account information: such as name, email address, user ID, or profile information if you create an account.
  • Product and scan data: such as barcode numbers, scanned product information, submitted product details, product images, ingredients images, reviews, questions, or safety feedback.
  • Device and technical data: such as device type, operating system, app version, crash logs, diagnostics, and performance information.
  • Camera and photo access: if you choose to capture or upload product or ingredient images.
  • Internet and usage data: such as app interactions, feature usage, and connection-related information required for app functionality.
  • Notification data: such as push notification tokens used to send app notifications, where enabled.

4. How We Use Information

ScanHa may use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve barcode scanning and product information features.
  • To display product, ingredient, allergen, dietary, and safety-related information.
  • To allow users to submit product information, reviews, questions, or feedback.
  • To manage user accounts and app access.
  • To send important app notifications, updates, or user-related alerts.
  • To monitor app performance, fix bugs, and improve reliability.
  • To protect the app from misuse, fraud, unauthorized activity, or policy violations.
  • To comply with legal, regulatory, or security obligations.

5. Medical and Allergy Disclaimer

ScanHa provides information based on available data sources, barcode scanning, user submissions, and product-related information. Product ingredients, allergen warnings, manufacturing methods, and packaging may change at any time without notice.

ScanHa does not guarantee that product information is always accurate, complete, current, or suitable for your specific health condition or dietary needs. You must always check the original product packaging before consuming or using any product.

If you have allergies, medical conditions, intolerances, or specific dietary requirements, you should consult a qualified healthcare professional. ScanHa is not a substitute for professional medical advice.

  • Safety and security: we may process or disclose information to protect users, prevent fraud, enforce terms, or secure the application.
  • User-submitted content: information you submit publicly, such as reviews, questions, feedback, or product contributions, may be visible to other users depending on the app feature.
